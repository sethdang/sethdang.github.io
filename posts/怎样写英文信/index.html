怎样写英文信

Seth

October 26, 2010

| 以下仅供网赚新朋友们参考：
范例信是你和公司打交道时的一个参考。这里的例子用词比较简单，也没有按写信的格式，但是清楚、明了。
◆ 帐号被删
◆ 收不到支票
◆ 统计不符
◆ 无法登陆
◆ 无法跑surfbar
◆ 取消帐户
网赚比较重要的英文范例信!
1.帐号被删：
My account is 帐户名 .I have receive company’s and said
that I have cheat!However, I have never cheat !Can you
help me to check about it?
Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是……我收到公司信说我作弊，但是我并没有作弊，请问你可不可以帮我检查一下？
谢谢你！
2.收不到支票：
My account is 帐户名 .I still have not got the cheque of 月
份！Can you help me to check about it?
Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是……我还没有收到X月的支票！请问你可不可以帮我检查一下？
谢谢你！
3.统计不符：
My account is 帐户名 .I should have $ X in my
account.However, there are only $Y,Can you help me to
check about it?
Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是……我应该有X元在帐户中，但是帐户只有Y元，请问你可不可以帮我检查一下？
谢谢你！
4.无法登陆：
My account is 帐 户 名 .I used to login everyday.However, i
cannot login today!Can you help me to check about it?
Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是……我平常可以正常登陆的，但是今天多次登陆，都无法成功！请问你可不可以帮我检查一下？
谢谢你！
5.无法跑surfbar：
My account is 帐 户 名 .I cannot use the viewbar,everytime
i use it ,it said XXXXX.What should i do?
Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是……我用不到viewbar，每次我用它时，它也说XXXXX。我应该怎样办？
谢谢你！
6.取消帐户：
My account is 帐 户 名 .My password is 密 码. I would like to
cancel my account.
Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是……我的密码是……我想取消帐户。
谢谢你！
常用的要求付款、催要付款等：

Here is the info
my id is wumeigirl ,my account balance: $******
Payment account My e-gold # is : *******
Please paid me as soon as possible

Have a nice day!

二）写信给老板催要付款
Greetings,
I have been waiting for so long to receive my
payment.What’s going on? Please check my account and pay
me in time to show your prestige. My username is
******，my e-gold accout is *******.
Thank you!
三）帐户无法登陆时写信给老板询问无法登陆的情况
Dear Admin,
My account was ******* and my email address registed
with your site is **********.
Today I could not log into my account and when I tried
to retrieve my password the system told me that my email
is not in the database. I am not sure whether it is
because of a system problem, my account being hacked or
my account being deleted.
Please help me check this up. Thanks.
四）帐户内统计出错写信给老板咨询具体情况
Dear sir:
My account is ******，I should have $ X in my 站长网,最新站长资讯
account.However, there are only $Y,Can you help me check
about it? Thank you for your kind attention!
我的帐户是。。。我应该有X元在帐户中，但是帐户只有Y元，请问你可不可以帮我检查一下？谢谢你！
五）帐户被删除，写信要回帐户
Greetings,
My account has been deleted with unknown reason,maybe I
clicked the cheat link, can not remember clearly, I am
busy and must be too tired these days. If so, I’m sorry,
pls accept my apology. I’m always a supportive and
honest member and have built many refs. I’m serious at
your prog,so pls let me know why account was
deleted.Thanks.My origin username is *******.
Thank you!
